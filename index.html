About the Conference

Welcome to the Ganga institute of technology and management 2025, a two-day premier event that brings together researchers, educators and practitioners to explore cutting-edge developments in computing and artificial intelligence. Join us in New Delhi, India on 15–16 November 2025 to share insights, network with peers and build collaborations that shape the future.
